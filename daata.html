About Annadaata

Our Story

Annadaata began as a small initiative to connect restaurants with surplus food to migrant workers with a wide network of thousands of volunteers.

Our name comes from the Sanskrit phrase "Annadaata Sukhibhava" meaning "May the food provider be blessed".
